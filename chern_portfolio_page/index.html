Hello

My name is Cherrnor, but I go by "Chern" (quite literally like a butter "churn") for short! I'm a Los Angeles based UX/UI designer, focused on unique story-telling, responsive design and creative expression in the digital world!


Social media has created an increasingly crowded echo chamber of similar voices, offering the same advice and services within niche markets. Design has given that imaginative child life, allowing me to create experiences that are unique and special.

I'm a student of experience, pulling from my diverse cultural background and adaptability, to create the best possible designs.
